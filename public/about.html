Inspired by the intersections of cognition, history, social engagement, and explosions; I found a sense of satisfaction working within the realm of art and design. Simultaneously, I am an optimist, humorist, enthusiast, explorer, thinker, and tinkerer. I’m particularly driven by the sensibilities and imagination of futurism, the unexpected eccentricity of the avante garde, and the poignant clarity of historical satire.

I firmly believe that the key to a full life is a wide vareity of experiences. In ordinance with this belief, I attempt to do and achieve as many things as possible. I am always pushing myself in new directions, attempting to tap into the knowledge of others, and continuously learn from the world around me. Actively pushing to reimagine the existing state of our world, my efforts are aimed at moving the existing towards the preferred.
